About

Our most recent project Chess: Holy War, aims to give the traditional and well known game of chess a fresh look, and at the same time, to make it more complex and interesting.

Our focus is developing a fun game, perfect for everyone, while still delighting every chess lover out there.

Responsive!

You might be wondering what does this mean... Easy! It means that you can enjoy the game on your PC at home... But you can also play it on your phone wherever and whenever you want.

Multiplayer

Chess Holy War is a local multiplayer game. You will be able to compete against your friends (for now) in the black and white field. But stay tuned... more cool stuff might be coming soon!

Lots of possibilities!

This game is way more than just chess... You can make every match unique building your own strategies by upgrading your pieces, equipping them with different objects, discovering advantageous tiles...

About Woody Race

Woody Race is a great racing game like you've never seen before. Race on yoour wooden horse as fast as you can while sorting crazy obstacles.

Many Worlds!

You will be able to race in different and beautiful thematic worlds like Block Town, the Card Palace, Chess World... And even practise until perfection in the hippodrome!

Betting system

But what really makes a difference apart from its joyful aesthetic is our betting system!! You are able to check every horse statistics and place a bet on your favourite and win some money.

Wii Balance Board

Tired of using the keyboard?? Try using the Wii Balance Board that you have at home collecting dust! This way you will feel as if you really were riding the wooden horsie.

Chess: Holy War beta version is out now!

After releasing its alpha version, we have been working nonstop to improve the game that ypu love so much, you can try it out now here.

Just by starting the game you can appreciate the changes in the game, such as a better looking and integrated UI, more detailed 3D pieces and a way more interesting scenario.

Also, we have introduced many new mechanics, such as random squares or an in-game store that provides you with different objects that you can equip ypur pieces with...

The new mechanics offer the player many possibilities, so for you to take advanteage of them we have implemented some tutorials.
